PhD in Information Engineering, BEng in Information Engineering, BSc in Mathematics (The Chinese University of Hong Kong)

Biography

David Ng completed his PhD degree in Information Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2021, under supervision of Prof. Chandra Nair. He obtained his Bachelor of Science in Mathematics (2015, first class honours) and Bachelor of Engineering in Information Engineering (2016, first class honours) both from The Chinese University of Hong Kong.
